Mudra overview

Mudras are ancient. They have significance in healing, religion, and dance.

Practicing hand mudras may have a beneficial effect on your physical health or emotional well-being.

Use them for immediate benefit or long-standing conditions.

Mudra practice

  • A quiet place is ideal.
  • Place your hands and fingers as shown.
  • Relax and press the fingers lightly together.
  • Hold the mudra with both hands for the suggested time.
  • Breathe calmly and slowly.
